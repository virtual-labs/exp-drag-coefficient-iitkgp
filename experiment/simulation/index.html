Formula Used

Frictional pressure drop

ΔP = \(\frac{fLρv^2}{2D}\) .......... eq (1)

Air flow rate

Q = \(Av\) (\(\frac{M^3}{s}\) ).......... eq (2)

Coefficient of friction (K)

\(\frac{fρ}{8}\) (\(Ns^2m^{-4}\)) .......... eq (3)

where,

f = Resistance coefficient

L = Length of the duct

ρ = Air density

v = Mean velocity

Instruction

  1. Click on the button to start the experiment.
  2. At first open the duct by clicking the button "Duct Open".
  3. Press the button "Add Tube" for placing the mine tube as obstacle in the duct.
  4. Then measure the dimension of the duct and obstacle area. To close the duct press the button "Close Duct".
  5. The corresponding pressure loss reading is taken with the help of Inclined Tube Manometer.
  6. Click on the throttle device to variate the air velocity.
  7. Then stop the anemometer traversing by clicking the button "stop Anemometer" and take the reading.
  8. After that add the result to the observation table by clicking the button "Add result"
  9. Repeat the process for all the throttle device.
  10. Then plot the graph by clicking on the button "Graph". After all the points are measured Click on "Plot Graph" and then click on "Best Fit Graph".
  11. Note: For real experiment- Duct width is between 0.34m and 0.36m; Duct Height is between 0.4m and 0.6m; Duct length is between 5.41m and 5.5m; and radius of duct is 0.25m.

The dimension of the duct and the tub is in the below input box.
